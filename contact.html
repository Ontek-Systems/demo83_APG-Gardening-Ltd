Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our landscaping, garden maintenance, and hardscaping services.

Still have questions?
How much experience does APG Gardening Ltd have?
We have been established for over 35 years. Under Arek's leadership, our highly skilled team brings decades of combined expertise to every landscaping and maintenance project we undertake.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily operate in Luton, Dunstable, Leighton Buzzard, St Albans, and the surrounding villages across Bedfordshire.
Do you provide regular garden maintenance?
Yes, we offer full service regular grounds upkeep. This includes precision lawn mowing, hedge cutting, specialized weed control, and systematic pruning to ensure your property looks its absolute best all year round.
Can you install new driveways and patios?
Absolutely. Our hard landscaping services cover the expert design and structural installation of high quality outdoor surfaces. We specialize in brick paving, Indian stone, porcelain patios, shingle paths, and highly durable resin driveways.
My garden is completely overgrown. Can you clear it out?
Yes, we provide high efficiency site clearance and restoration services. We can clear severely overgrown plots, remove green waste, and use high pressure washing to restore stone and concrete surfaces to their former glory.
Why should I choose APG Gardening Ltd for my landscaping project?
With an extensive base of loyal clients, we proudly retain over 75 percent of our customers long term. We balance modern operational standards with affordable pricing, ensuring you receive premium quality workmanship without the inflated costs.
